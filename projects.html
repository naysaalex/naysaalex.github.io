Estee Lauder Companies Accessible Beauty Hackathon

The prompt for this hackathon was to design a software/system that would make beauty more accessible. Our team designed an all-in-one platform where customers with disabilities would be able to communicate their specific needs with employees at beauty establishments. The platform also created the opportunity for beauty employees to learn more about how to accommodate for specific disabilities through certified courses. Our team created our designs and application prototype using Figma.

Coding languages/Software used: Google Forms, Figma, Canva
